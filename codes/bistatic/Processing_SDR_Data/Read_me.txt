Purpose of Code:
To create coherent and incoherent sums of all data in a folder


Directions for use of code:
1) Put all data from a single measurement in a folder with nothing else in it.
2) Rename all files to end in .dat
3) Open coherentIncoherent_sum_all_in_folder_ver2.m
4) Change "dataFolder" to be the directory for the folder where all your data is. 
5) Change "myTitle" to what you want the figures to be saved as.
6) Run the code, and figures and mat files will be saved to your current directory.
